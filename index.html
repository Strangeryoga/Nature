C o s m o s

Cosmos often simply means "universe". But the word is generally used

to suggest an orderly or harmonious universe, as it was originally used

by Pythagoras in the 6th century B.C. Thus, a religious mystic may help

put us in touch with the cosmos, and so may a physicist.
